Refund & Return Policy

1. Returns

If the products are defective at the time of delivery or damaged, they can only be returned.

2. Refund Eligibility

After verifying the returned product condition, only refunds are processed.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Non-refundable indicates that the products purchased are in good condition.
